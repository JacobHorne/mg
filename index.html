Firm Overview

In April 1997, J. R. Murphy and John M. Grantland founded the firm along with the help of a paralegal and receptionist. With the mission of providing high-quality legal services with uncompromising integrity and uncommon value, Murphy and Grantland , P.A. flourished and more than doubled its client base and workload. Over the next decade, Murphy and Grantland, P.A. expanded to include over a dozen attorneys, and a full-fledged legal and office support staff. After outgrowing three different office locations, Murphy and Grantland, P.A. built its present location at 4406-B Forest Drive in Columbia in 2005.

About

Murphy and Grantland, P.A. offers lawyers with a diversity of legal experience with specialists in a variety of practice areas including: Insurance Coverage and Bad Faith, Premises Liability, Insurance Defense, Trucking Defense, Products Liability, Construction Defects, Employment Law, Medical and Professional Malpractice Defense and Workers' Compensation.
